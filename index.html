How to play?

✍️
Level letter

Each level introduces a new letter that all search words must contain. It can be at the beginning, middle, or end of the word.

⚡
Zigzag layout

Words are arranged along broken paths — each next letter can be in any of the 8 adjacent cells.

💡
Hints

Find 3 words not from the current level's word list to get a hint: one of the required words will be highlighted on the grid.

🏆
Goal

To complete a level, you need to find all the hidden words. Try to use as few hints as possible!
